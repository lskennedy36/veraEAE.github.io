Auto Clicker (Using Arduino UNO)

Physical auto clicker for games

In October 2022, i came across a halloween event that requires me to click on the mouse whenever when the character is apple bobbing.

The apple is only caught randomly and when that happens, a loud audio would play.

I have tried an auto-clicker program online but it got me banned instead. So i thought it would be a good idea to make a physical autoclicker, also to help me learn

I had used a servomotor, microphone and the arduino

I had decided to use a microphone since a constant auto clicking could possibly get me banned from the game again.

Once the microphone detects sound, the bulb would light up and then the servomotor would click the mouse after 3 seconds

Unfortunately, i did not improve the design further, and just used a ruler to hold the motor in place.

I used Arduino IDE to create the program for this using C++.

With this auto-clicker, i could efficiently play the game without being there. My game could run for hours without failure.
